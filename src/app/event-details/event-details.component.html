Our foundation is deeply committed to promoting sports and outdoor activities among children, recognizing the urgent need to counter the adverse effects of excessive screen time. In today’s digital age, where children are increasingly engrossed in smartphones, tablets, and video games, there has been a noticeable decline in physical activity, social interaction, and overall well-being . To address this growing concern, we have made it our mission to introduce new sports and activities, encouraging children to engage in outdoor play while developing healthy lifestyle habits that will benefit them for years to come.

A key revelation from one of our recent Mega Health Check-up Camps reinforced the importance of this initiative. During the camp, we discovered that around 40 children were suffering from low vision, a worrying statistic that highlights the negative impact of prolonged screen exposure on children’s eyesight. This discovery has strengthened our resolve to implement structured programs aimed at motivating children to participate in physical activities tailored to their abilities. By providing access to adaptive sports and inclusive games, we seek to enhance their coordination, self-confidence, and overall health, while simultaneously reducing their dependency on digital screens.

We can set up this initiative at our own cost or at the cost of our association and involve local rights in it. This approach will create greater awareness among people, as 10,000 players are expected to participate. Therefore, it is crucial for us to establish a strong local presence in our area. To achieve this, we have already taken the initial steps with a few organizations. In Delhi, we have access to a renowned park, and we are receiving calls regarding this initiative through DDA (Delhi Development Authority). When DDA takes the initiative , it sends a message to the local community, informing them that this sport has been implemented in a major DDA park. This not only lends credibility to the sport but also ensures that residents become aware of it, regardless of whether they actively participate. Over time, as activity in the park increases, local players, sports enthusiasts, and individuals engaged in other games will take an interest and begin participating.

Beyond the obvious physical benefits of sports, participation in games and athletic activities also nurtures important life skills such as teamwork, discipline, perseverance, and resilience. Engaging in structured sports programs helps children build social connections, enhance communication skills, and learn to work towards common goals—skills that are essential for personal and professional success. Additionally, as these students and players progress, it is vital that they receive sponsorships and financial support at later stages when they begin competing in larger events. Hence, we should focus on expanding this initiative beyond the local level, ensuring that the sport gains wider recognition and long-term sustainability.

Our ultimate goal is to bring about a positive transformation in children's lives by shifting their focus from passive screen consumption to active participation in sports and outdoor play. Through consistent efforts, strong community partnerships, and parental involvement , we envision a future where children grow up healthier, happier, and more connected to their surroundings. By instilling the values of active living and sportsmanship, we can create a movement that champions physical activity, ensuring that future generations are strong, confident, and ready to embrace life’s opportunities with enthusiasm and determination.

On 1st July 2024, Shri Nanak Foundation organized a Mega Health Checkup Camp at Bahubali Park, Karkardooma Road, Bahubali Enclave, Anand Vihar, Delhi-92 . This initiative was aimed at providing free and accessible healthcare services to individuals from all walks of life. With a vision to promote community well-being and preventive healthcare , the event was conducted in collaboration with Indraprastha Apollo Hospital the camp brought together experienced doctors, medical specialists, and healthcare professionals who extended their expertise to those in need.

The health camp offered a wide range of medical services, including general physician consultation, blood pressure monitoring, blood sugar tests, cholesterol screenings, eye and dental checkups, and BMI assessments . Special attention was given to elderly individuals, women, and children, ensuring that their specific health concerns were addressed. Additionally, free consultations with specialist doctors from various fields such as cardiology, orthopedics, gynecology, and general medicine were provided to attendees.

One of the highlights of the camp was the awareness and counseling sessions, where participants were educated about healthy lifestyle choices, disease prevention, and proper nutrition. Doctors and medical professionals also spoke about the importance of early detection in preventing serious health conditions. The camp emphasized the need for regular health checkups, encouraging individuals to adopt a proactive approach to their well-being.

To further support the community, free medicines and essential health supplements were distributed among those in need. The initiative also included eye checkups with provisions for free spectacles for individuals diagnosed with vision impairments. Special screenings were conducted for common lifestyle diseases such as diabetes, hypertension, and heart ailments , which have become prevalent due to modern-day sedentary habits.

The response to the health camp was overwhelming, with a large number of people benefiting from the services provided. The event witnessed active participation from volunteers, social workers, and healthcare professionals , all working together towards the common goal of serving the community. The atmosphere was filled with gratitude and appreciation, as beneficiaries expressed their heartfelt thanks for receiving quality healthcare services free of cost.

Shri Nanak Foundation remains committed to enhancing healthcare accessibility and awareness within the community. This Mega Health Checkup Camp was a significant step in that direction, and we look forward to organizing more such impactful initiatives in the future. Through these efforts, we aim to build a healthier society, where everyone has the opportunity to lead a life of wellness, dignity, and happiness

On 21st January 2024, our foundation trust organized a grand Shobha Yatra, celebrating the enthusiasm and devotion of the people of Delhi regarding the installation of Lord Shri Ram in Ayodhya. The procession was a majestic and spiritually uplifting event, bringing together hundreds of devotees who expressed their reverence and excitement through active participation. The event was hosted at the Shri Ram Temple in Surajmal Vihar, East Delhi , under the aegis of Shri Nanak Foundation, and witnessed an outpouring of devotion and faith.

The Shobha Yatra featured a series of beautifully decorated tableaus, depicting key scenes and characters from the Ramayana, including Lord Shri Ram, Mata Sita, Lakshman, Hanuman, and other divine figures. These artistic representations captivated the audience and provided a unique visual storytelling experience. The streets of East Delhi came alive with the chanting of bhajans, devotional songs, and slogans in praise of Lord Ram, creating a truly spiritual atmosphere.

During the event, CA Ankit Gupta was quoted in the report, highlighting that the procession provided a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity for devotees to witness Lord Ram, Sita, and Lakshman in a grand spectacle. Additionally, Foundation President CA Liril Bansal spoke about the significance of the Yatra, emphasizing its role in fostering cultural and spiritual unity among the people. The procession followed a well-planned route, covering key locations in East Delhi, including Anand Vihar, Manak Vihar, Ashoka Niketan, and Jhilmil, before culminating at the Pashupatinath Temple in Vishwas Nagar.

The event not only served as a spiritual journey for devotees but also as a symbol of unity, tradition, and faith . Many participants expressed their joy at being part of such a historic moment, which strengthened their connection to the rich cultural and religious heritage of India. The Yatra was also accompanied by traditional music, devotional dance performances, and special prayers , adding to the grandeur of the occasion.

The widespread media coverage further highlighted the success and significance of the event. Reports were published in prominent newspapers, including Punjab Kesari (Page 10, 24th January 2024), Amar Ujala (Page 5, 23rd January 2024), and Hindustan (Page 6, 23rd January 2024) , all of which captured the essence of the celebration and the overwhelming excitement of both participants and onlookers.

This Shobha Yatra was a testament to the deep-rooted devotion of the people and their unwavering faith in Lord Shri Ram . The overwhelming response and enthusiasm displayed during the event have encouraged us to continue organizing such religious and cultural events , fostering a spirit of unity, devotion, and tradition. We look forward to many more such grand celebrations in the future, ensuring that the legacy of Lord Ram and our cultural heritage continues to shine brightly.

On December 17, 2023, our organization successfully organized a Mega Health Checkup Camp, reinforcing our unwavering commitment to community health and well-being. This initiative aimed to provide essential healthcare services to individuals who may not have easy access to quality medical facilities. We are proud to share that the camp catered to the health needs of more than 250 individuals, ensuring they received timely medical attention and guidance for a healthier future.

The event was conducted in collaboration with Indraprastha Apollo Hospital, with an expert team of healthcare professionals and specialists extending their services to the community. A wide range of free health checkups and screenings were provided, covering essential diagnostic tests such as blood pressure monitoring, blood glucose testing, cholesterol screening, BMI assessment, vision checkups, dental screenings, and general health consultations. These tests were crucial in detecting potential health risks at an early stage and providing necessary medical advice.

In addition to screenings, free consultations with specialist doctors from various fields, including cardiology, endocrinology, orthopedics, and general medicine, were made available to attendees. Patients had the opportunity to discuss their health concerns, seek expert opinions, and receive valuable guidance on managing existing medical conditions. To further support the community, we also distributed free medicines to those in need, ensuring that they had access to the necessary medications to manage their health conditions effectively.

Beyond medical checkups, our team focused on health awareness and education, offering valuable insights on maintaining a balanced diet, regular exercise, stress management, and preventive healthcare practices. Educational sessions were conducted to promote healthy lifestyle habits, empowering individuals to take charge of their well-being and make informed health decisions.

This event would not have been possible without the unwavering support of our dedicated healthcare professionals, volunteers, and community partners, who worked tirelessly to ensure the camp's success. Their efforts played a crucial role in making quality healthcare accessible to those who needed it the most. We sincerely appreciate their hard work, commitment, and compassion in serving the community.

As we reflect on the impact of this Mega Health Checkup Camp, we remain committed to our mission of enhancing community health and wellness. We look forward to organizing more such initiatives in the future, with the vision of making healthcare affordable, accessible, and inclusive for all. Together, we can build a healthier and stronger community, ensuring a better quality of life for everyone.
