Shri Nanak Foundation

Shri Nanak Foundation is a Non - Profit Organization (NPO), is a beacon of hope for underprivileged communities. Impacting lifes of 1000+ children and their families. We have various projects on free health camps, promoting physical and mental well-being through the practice of yoga, distributing blankets - offering warmth and comfort to those most vulnerable to the cold promoting social well-being, sports camps. The response and co-operation Shri Nanak Foundation is receiving, motivates us to work even harder to plan and execute different projects. Through these multifaceted initiatives, the Shri Nanak Foundation uplifts lives, fostering not just physical well-being, but also a sense of hope and opportunity.
