Shri Nanak Foundation

Shri Nanak Foundation is a beacon of hope, dedicated to transforming lives and communities through compassionate service and sustainable initiatives. Rooted in the principles of universal well-being, the foundation strives to address the multifaceted needs of society, focusing particularly on the empowerment of marginalized populations. By championing accessible education, comprehensive healthcare, and robust social welfare programs, Shri Nanak Foundation aims to cultivate a society where every individual has the opportunity to flourish. Furthermore, the foundation's commitment to environmental stewardship and the preservation of cultural heritage underscores its holistic approach to creating a more just, harmonious, and sustainable future for all.

Vision

लोककल्याण
To create a just and equitable society where all individuals have the opportunity to thrive, fostering a world of harmony, well-being, and sustainable development.

Mission

आरोग्य दानम् - योगक्षेमं वहाम्यहम्
To empower and uplift underserved communities through comprehensive initiatives in education, healthcare, social welfare, and environmental sustainability, fostering a just and harmonious society.

Motto

सर्वजन हिताय: सर्वजन सुखाय:
"Sarbat da Bhala" (Well-being for all) - reflecting the core principle of universal welfare and service to humanity.

Trustee, CA Liril Bansal

CA Liril Bansal's journey is a testament to the power of perseverance and the spirit of giving back. Rising from humble beginnings, he dedicated himself to rigorous study, overcoming numerous obstacles to achieve his dream of becoming a Chartered Accountant. Now, with a heart full of compassion and a deep understanding of the challenges faced by the underserved, Liril is channeling his success into the Shri Nanak Foundation. This charitable trust embodies his unwavering commitment to creating opportunities for others, reflecting his belief that everyone deserves a chance to build a better future, regardless of their starting point. His personal story fuels the foundation's mission, driving its efforts to empower communities and uplift lives through education, healthcare, and compassionate service.
