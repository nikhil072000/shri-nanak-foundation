Free Tally Training Program by Shri Nanak Foundation

Empowering Youth, Strengthening India – A Step Towards Viksit Bharat

Shri Nanak Foundation is proud to launch a Free Tally Training Program, aligned with our Hon’ble Prime Minister’s vision of Viksit Bharat, ensuring that no one remains unemployed. In today’s fast-evolving financial landscape, accounting skills are crucial for businesses, startups, and professionals. Through this initiative, we aim to equip individuals with essential Tally skills, making them job-ready and financially independent.
